CRITICAL RAG INSIGHT
Employment ≠ Labour
This is one of the most important distinctions.
Many firms:
are strong in employment advisory
but weak in labour relations
Others:
dominate union negotiations
but do little executive employment work
RankPilot MUST distinguish:
employment sophistication
 from:
labour sophistication

 ❌ confuse union-side with employer-side

IDEAL CHAMBERS LABOUR REASONING
Strong diagnostics should sound like:
✅ “the submission demonstrates strong workforce restructuring sophistication”
✅ “the practice appears highly credible in collective bargaining and labour board proceedings”
✅ “the matters show strong employer-side advisory depth but limited strategic litigation exposure”
✅ “the submission aligns more naturally with employer-side employment than labour relations”
✅ “the narrative suggests regional labour dominance rather than nationwide capability”
