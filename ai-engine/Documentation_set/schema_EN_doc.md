## Overview
Defines two Pydantic models for structuring law firm practice area submissions: a `Matter` model for individual legal cases and a `SubmissionSchema` model for the complete submission data.

## Classes
| Class | Primary Duty |
|-------|--------------|
| `Matter` | Represents a specific legal case or 'matter' from the submission, capturing its strategic details and jurisdictional scope. |
| `SubmissionSchema` | The full structured representation of a law firm's practice submission, aggregating firm details and a list of matters. |
